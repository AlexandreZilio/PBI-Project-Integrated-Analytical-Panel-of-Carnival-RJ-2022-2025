# Carnival RJ – Integrated Analytical Panel (2022–2025)
### Power BI Dashboard – Operational, Economic, Structural, and Social Analysis of the Event

This repository contains all the documentation, data, and visualizations related to the Analytical Panel of Rio de Janeiro’s Carnival (2022–2025). The project aims to provide a consolidated view of the main operational pillars of Carnival, organizing strategic information clearly, in an integrated manner, and focused on supporting decision-making.

---

## Project Overview

The dashboard was developed in Power BI and presents comprehensive analyses on:

### Mobilized Workforce
- City Hall personnel  
- Sambadrome workers  
- Licensed street vendors  
- COMLURB cleaning crews  
- Inspectors and support teams  
- Total workforce evolution between 2022 and 2025  

### Municipal Agencies Involved
- Participation of 20 to 23 municipal agencies per year  
- Actions segmented by phases (planning, pre-Carnival, and operations)  
- Total staff allocated to each edition  

### Economic Indicators
- Estimated economic activity each year  
- Direct and indirect jobs generated  
- Participants and tourists (domestic and international)  
- Total street parties and hours of parades per year  

### Infrastructure and Operational Structure
- Cleaning and waste collection equipment  
- Operational vehicles  
- Surveillance cameras  
- LED projectors  
- Distribution by service areas (collection, cleaning, Sambadrome, and surroundings)  

### Samba Schools – Categories and Members
- Analysis of the Special Group, Série Ouro (Gold Tier), and Children’s Schools  
- Total number of participants  
- Number of parade days  
- Operational growth at the Sambadrome over the years  

### Street Carnival Blocs – Typology and Timeline
- Comparison between pre-Carnival and official week  
- Analysis by bloc category (traditional, mega-blocs, etc.)  
- Annual trends and total volume of permits issued  

---

## Objectives

The dashboard was designed to:
- Centralize operational information about Carnival  
- Support planning and team management  
- Track economic, structural, and social changes over the years  
- Provide transparency and reliable data for institutional reporting  
- Enable managerial and comparative analyses between different editions

## Key Insights Identified

- Continuous growth of economic impact (from **R$ 4B** to **R$ 5.7B**)  
- Expansion of operational and technological infrastructure  
- Growth of total mobilized workforce  
- Increase in participant and tourist flows  
- Balanced and rising distribution of authorized street blocs  
- Growth in the number of samba school participants
